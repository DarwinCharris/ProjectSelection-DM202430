Information about the dataset

The Loan Defaulter dataset available on Kaggle is a valuable tool for a data mining project. This dataset contains detailed information about loans and customers, allowing us students to practice building predictive models to identify customers at risk of defaulting. This skill is crucial for financial institutions, as it improves decision-making and reduces the risks associated with lending. Additionally, working with this dataset allows for the application of advanced techniques such as classification, regression, and data analysis, fostering a deep understanding of data mining methods.

Integrating this dataset into our academic project not only provides hands-on experience in manipulating real data, but also prepares us students for real-world challenges, where risk prediction and management are critical.

This dataset has 122 columns, some of its most interesting tags are:

TARGET: Target variable (1 - client with payment difficulties: he/she had late payment more than X days on at least one of the first Y installments of the loan in our sample, 0 - all other cases)
OCCUPATION_TYPE: What kind of occupation does the client have
CODE_GENDER: Gender of the client
FLAG_OWN_CAR: Flag if the client owns a car
FLAG_OWN_REALTY: Flag if client owns a house or flat
CNT_CHILDREN: Number of children the client has
AMT_INCOME_TOTAL: Income of the client
NAME_INCOME_TYPE: Clients income type (businessman, working, maternity leave,â€¦)
NAME_EDUCATION_TYPE: Level of highest education the client achieved
NAME_FAMILY_STATUS: Family status of the client
NAME_HOUSING_TYPE: What is the housing situation of the client (renting, living with parents, ...)
AMT_CREDIT: Credit amount of the loan
DAYS_BIRTH: Client's age in days at the time of application
DAYS_EMPLOYED: How many days before the application the person started current employment
